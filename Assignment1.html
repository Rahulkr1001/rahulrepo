Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Reeve Musk FRS is a business magnate and investor.He was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, one of South Africa's capital cities. He is the founder, CEO and chief engineer of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; owner and CEO of Twitter, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI;According to Elon Musk, he is working on a plan to settle humans on Mars by 2030. In 2003, Elon Musk, together with 2 people, founded a company called Tesla Ink. He has been serving as the Chief executive officer of Tesla since 2008. He was the largest share-holder of PayPal.
